## Troubleshooting

### Connection Failed
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure I gave you the correct stream URL
- Ask me if I restarted the server

### Choppy/Laggy Video
- Check your internet speed (need at least 10 Mbps)
- Try lowering the network caching in VLC settings
- Close other programs using bandwidth
